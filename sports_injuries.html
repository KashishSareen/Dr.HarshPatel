Understanding Sports Injuries

What Are Sports Injuries?

Sports injuries are injuries that occur during athletic activities or exercise. They can range from acute trauma like ACL tears and fractures to overuse conditions like tendinitis and stress fractures.

Dr. Harsh Patel specializes in diagnosing and treating all types of sports-related musculoskeletal conditions, helping athletes of all levels return to their sport safely and quickly.

Rotator cuff tears and shoulder injuries
Meniscus tears and cartilage damage
