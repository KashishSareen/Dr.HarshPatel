Your Trusted Orthopedic Partner

Dr. Harsh Patel is a highly skilled orthopedic surgeon based in Bhayandar, Mumbai. With over a decade of specialized experience, he has helped thousands of patients regain mobility, reduce pain, and live fuller lives.

He specializes in complex joint replacement surgeries, including robotic-assisted knee replacement, arthroscopic procedures, and sports injury management. Committed to providing world-class orthopedic care across Bhayandar, Mira Road, and Greater Mumbai.
