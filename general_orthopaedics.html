Understanding General Orthopaedics

What is General Orthopaedics?

General orthopaedics encompasses the diagnosis, treatment, and management of a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions. Dr. Patel provides comprehensive OPD consultations for all bone, joint, and muscle problems.

From back pain and neck pain to shoulder problems, arthritis, and bone deformities, Dr. Patel offers expert evaluation and personalized treatment plans for every patient.

The Consultation Journey

How Our OPD Consultations Work

Step 01

Comprehensive Evaluation

Dr. Patel takes a detailed medical history and performs a thorough physical examination to understand your condition.

Step 02

Diagnostic Imaging

If needed, X-rays, MRI, or CT scans are ordered to confirm the diagnosis and assess the severity of your condition.

Step 03

Personalized Treatment Plan

A customized treatment plan is developed, which may include medications, injections, physiotherapy, or surgery if needed.

Step 04

Follow-Up & Monitoring

Regular follow-up visits to monitor your progress and adjust treatment as needed for optimal outcomes.
